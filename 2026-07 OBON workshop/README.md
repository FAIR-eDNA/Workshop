## OBON FAIR eDNA workshop (online)
A joint online workshop from [OBON](https://obon-ocean.org/), FAIRe, [BeBOP](https://bebop-obon.github.io/), and [OBIS](https://obis.org/) that teaches OBON community members how to make eDNA data, protocols, and workflows FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, Reusable).

This workshop was made possible by multiple contributors.
- Organisers/maintainers: Bayden Willms, Kathleen Pitz, Luke Thompson, Miwa Takahashi, Saara Suominen  
- OBON Data Synthesis Working Group leads: Jennifer Sunday, Jodie van de Kamp
- OBON: Fiona Beckman, Luz Rodriguez‑Vargas, Sophie Seeyave
